# Payment Scenarios Reference / Rujukan Senario Pembayaran

This document lists every sales-invoice / payment / receipt / bank-in scenario supported by the ERP after the Phase 1–8 accounting refactor (see `INVOICE_PAYMENT_ACCOUNTING_PROGRESS.md`), what it means in plain terms, how the system records it, and which journal accounts/codes it posts to.

Dokumen ini menyenaraikan setiap senario invois jualan / bayaran / resit / bank-in yang disokong oleh sistem ERP selepas pembaikan perakaunan Fasa 1–8 (rujuk `INVOICE_PAYMENT_ACCOUNTING_PROGRESS.md`), maksudnya dalam bahasa mudah, cara sistem merekodkannya, dan akaun/kod jurnal yang terlibat.

---

## English

| # | Scenario | What it is | How it's recorded in the ERP | Journal posting (DR / CR) |
|---|----------|------------|-------------------------------|----------------------------|
| 1 | **Cash bill (invoice paid at point of sale)** | Customer buys and pays in full immediately; no outstanding balance. | Invoice created with `paymenttype = 'cash'`. The system auto-generates a non-posting "auto-collection" `payments` row (`is_auto_collection = true`) dated to the invoice's local date, so the invoice appears settled on the same day without the user doing anything. | One journal, 4 lines: DR customer debtor-child (sale) / CR CASH_SALES (sale) / DR CH_REV1 (auto-collection, cash received) / CR customer debtor-child (auto-collection, settles the sale) |
| 2 | **Banking cash sales (CH_REV1 → bank)** | The day's till cash from cash bills is physically deposited into the bank. | User goes to Accounting → Cash Bank-In (RV), selects a CH_REV1 date-pool (or partial amount from it), enters/reserves an RV number, and posts. | DR BANK_PBB (or chosen bank account) / CR CH_REV1 |
| 3 | **Credit sale (invoice on account, unpaid)** | Customer buys on credit; nothing collected yet. | Invoice created with `paymenttype = 'credit'` (previously called "invoice"/"credit" type). No auto-collection row is created. | DR customer debtor-child / CR CR_SALES |
| 4 | **Paying a credit invoice in cash** | Customer later pays cash against an earlier credit invoice. | User records a receipt via Payment Form / Payment Page with `payment_method = 'cash'`. This creates a `receipts` header + `receipt_allocations` row(s), immediately posted (cash needs no clearing). | DR CH_REV2 (one line per invoice allocation, referenced `C{invoice_id}`) / CR customer debtor-child |
| 5 | **Banking that collected cash (CH_REV2 → bank)** | The cash collected in scenario 4 is later deposited. | Same Cash Bank-In (RV) flow as #2, but the source is one or more CH_REV2 receipts instead of a CH_REV1 pool. | DR BANK_PBB (or chosen bank account) / CR CH_REV2 |
| 6 | **Bank transfer / online payment received** | Customer pays directly into the company's bank account (transfer, FPX, etc.) — money is already "in the bank," nothing to physically deposit. | Receipt recorded with `payment_method = 'bank_transfer'` or `'online'`; posted immediately since there's no clearing delay. | DR BANK_PBB / BANK_ABB (aggregated one line per receipt) / CR customer debtor-child(ren) |
| 7 | **Cheque received (pending clearance)** | Customer pays by cheque; the amount isn't usable/confirmed until the cheque clears. | Receipt created with `payment_method = 'cheque'`, `status = 'pending'`, `posting_date = NULL`. Nothing is posted yet — the invoice stays open. When the cheque clears, the user calls "confirm," which sets `posting_date` to the clearing date and posts the journal then. | On confirm: DR BANK_PBB (or bank account) / CR customer debtor-child |
| 8 | **Overpayment / excess payment** | Customer pays more than the invoice(s) they're settling. | The extra amount is recorded as an `excess` allocation on the same receipt, owned by the customer (not tied to a specific invoice) and stored in `CUST_DEP` (customer deposit) for later use or refund. | DR cash/bank account (full amount received) / CR customer debtor-child (invoice portion) + CR CUST_DEP (excess portion) |
| 9 | **Zero-value bill** | A cash or credit invoice with a total of RM0.00 (e.g. fully discounted, sample, or FOC-only bill). | Still generates a journal for audit-trail completeness, but with all amounts at 0.00 — an informational entry rather than a real movement. | DR customer debtor-child 0.00 / CR CASH_SALES or CR_SALES 0.00 |
| 10 | **Credit Note (CN)** | Reduces what the customer owes on a specific invoice — e.g. returned goods, pricing correction, discount given after the fact. | Adjustment document of `type = 'credit_note'`, linked to the `original_invoice_id`. The system checks the accounting identity (`excl-tax + rounding + tax = payable`) holds before posting. | DR the ORIGINAL invoice's revenue ledger (CR_SALES if credit invoice, CASH_SALES if cash invoice) + DR OUTPUT_TAX (if tax > 0) / CR customer debtor-child |
| 11 | **Debit Note (DN)** | Increases what the customer owes on a specific invoice — e.g. under-billing correction, additional charge. | Adjustment document of `type = 'debit_note'`; exact reversal of a CN's postings. | DR customer debtor-child / CR the ORIGINAL invoice's revenue ledger + CR OUTPUT_TAX (if tax > 0) |
| 12 | **Refund Note (RN) — paired with a Credit Note** | Money is physically refunded to the customer for a CN already issued (rather than leaving it as unused credit). | Adjustment document of `type = 'refund_note'` with `paired_with_id` pointing at the CN. Never touches revenue — it only moves cash/bank out and clears the CN's effect on the debtor balance. | DR customer debtor-child / CR CASH or BANK account (per `refund_method`/`bank_account`) |
| 13 | **Refund Note (RN) — standalone, against an overpayment** | Customer previously overpaid (scenario 8) and now wants the excess refunded in cash/bank instead of using it against future invoices. | Standalone RN with `linked_payment_id` pointing at the original overpaid receipt/payment. The corresponding `receipt_allocations.refunded_amount` is incremented so the excess balance reflects the refund. | DR CUST_DEP (excess) / CR CASH or BANK account |
| 14 | **Payment-type conversion (credit ↔ cash)** | User changes an invoice from credit to cash or vice-versa after creation (rare, but supported for corrections). | The invoice's journal is cancelled and rebuilt to match the new `paymenttype`'s contract (2-line for credit, up to 4-line for cash); any auto-collection row is created/removed to match. | Rebuilt per Scenario 1 or 3 above, depending on the new type |
| 15 | **Manual bank entries not tied to any invoice** | Miscellaneous cash movements through the bank — e.g. worker loan repayment received, vendor refund received, bank charges, interest — that don't relate to a sales invoice. | Recorded as a manual/miscellaneous RV entry in the shared `rv_registry` (same numbering pool as structured Cash Bank-Ins), or as a standalone manual journal entry (`entry_type = 'J'`) if no RV number is needed. | DR/CR bank account against whatever account code the transaction actually affects (e.g. DR BANK_PBB / CR staff loan account) |

## Bahasa Melayu

| # | Senario | Apa itu | Cara direkod dalam ERP | Catatan jurnal (DR / CR) |
|---|---------|---------|------------------------|---------------------------|
| 1 | **Bil tunai (invois dibayar semasa jualan)** | Pelanggan membeli dan membayar penuh serta-merta; tiada baki tertunggak. | Invois dicipta dengan `paymenttype = 'cash'`. Sistem secara automatik menjana baris "auto-collection" `payments` yang tidak diposkan (`is_auto_collection = true`) bertarikh sama dengan tarikh tempatan invois, supaya invois kelihatan selesai pada hari yang sama tanpa tindakan pengguna. | Satu jurnal, 4 baris: DR anak akaun debtor pelanggan (jualan) / CR CASH_SALES (jualan) / DR CH_REV1 (auto-collection, tunai diterima) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan (auto-collection, selesaikan jualan) |
| 2 | **Bank-in jualan tunai (CH_REV1 → bank)** | Wang tunai till hari itu daripada bil tunai dimasukkan secara fizikal ke bank. | Pengguna pergi ke Accounting → Cash Bank-In (RV), pilih kumpulan tarikh CH_REV1 (atau sebahagian jumlahnya), masukkan/tempah nombor RV, dan pos. | DR BANK_PBB (atau akaun bank dipilih) / CR CH_REV1 |
| 3 | **Jualan kredit (invois akaun, belum dibayar)** | Pelanggan membeli secara kredit; belum ada apa-apa dikutip. | Invois dicipta dengan `paymenttype = 'credit'`. Tiada baris auto-collection dicipta. | DR anak akaun debtor pelanggan / CR CR_SALES |
| 4 | **Membayar invois kredit secara tunai** | Pelanggan kemudian membayar tunai untuk invois kredit terdahulu. | Pengguna merekod resit melalui Payment Form / Payment Page dengan `payment_method = 'cash'`. Ini mencipta header `receipts` + baris `receipt_allocations`, terus diposkan (tunai tidak perlu kelulusan/clearing). | DR CH_REV2 (satu baris bagi setiap peruntukan invois, rujukan `C{invoice_id}`) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan |
| 5 | **Bank-in wang tunai yang dikutip (CH_REV2 → bank)** | Tunai yang dikutip dalam senario 4 kemudiannya dimasukkan ke bank. | Aliran Cash Bank-In (RV) yang sama seperti #2, tetapi sumbernya ialah satu atau lebih resit CH_REV2 dan bukan kumpulan CH_REV1. | DR BANK_PBB (atau akaun bank dipilih) / CR CH_REV2 |
| 6 | **Pindahan bank / bayaran online diterima** | Pelanggan membayar terus ke akaun bank syarikat (pindahan, FPX, dll.) — wang sudah "berada di bank," tiada apa-apa perlu dimasukkan secara fizikal. | Resit direkod dengan `payment_method = 'bank_transfer'` atau `'online'`; diposkan serta-merta kerana tiada tempoh kelulusan. | DR BANK_PBB / BANK_ABB (dikumpul satu baris bagi setiap resit) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan |
| 7 | **Cek diterima (menunggu kelulusan)** | Pelanggan membayar melalui cek; jumlah itu tidak boleh digunakan/disahkan sehingga cek itu clear. | Resit dicipta dengan `payment_method = 'cheque'`, `status = 'pending'`, `posting_date = NULL`. Tiada apa-apa diposkan lagi — invois kekal terbuka. Apabila cek clear, pengguna memilih "confirm," yang menetapkan `posting_date` kepada tarikh clear dan jurnal diposkan pada masa itu. | Semasa confirm: DR BANK_PBB (atau akaun bank) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan |
| 8 | **Bayaran lebih (overpayment)** | Pelanggan membayar lebih daripada invois yang diselesaikan. | Jumlah lebihan direkod sebagai peruntukan `excess` pada resit yang sama, dimiliki oleh pelanggan (tidak terikat kepada invois tertentu) dan disimpan dalam `CUST_DEP` (deposit pelanggan) untuk kegunaan atau bayaran balik kemudian. | DR akaun tunai/bank (jumlah penuh diterima) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan (bahagian invois) + CR CUST_DEP (bahagian lebihan) |
| 9 | **Bil bernilai sifar** | Invois tunai atau kredit dengan jumlah RM0.00 (contohnya diskaun penuh, sampel, atau bil FOC sahaja). | Tetap menjana jurnal untuk kelengkapan jejak audit, tetapi semua jumlah pada 0.00 — catatan bermaklumat sahaja, bukan pergerakan sebenar. | DR anak akaun debtor pelanggan 0.00 / CR CASH_SALES atau CR_SALES 0.00 |
| 10 | **Nota Kredit (CN)** | Mengurangkan jumlah yang perlu dibayar pelanggan pada invois tertentu — contohnya barang dipulangkan, pembetulan harga, diskaun diberi selepas fakta. | Dokumen pelarasan jenis `type = 'credit_note'`, dipautkan kepada `original_invoice_id`. Sistem menyemak identiti perakaunan (`tidak termasuk cukai + rounding + cukai = perlu dibayar`) dipatuhi sebelum diposkan. | DR lejar hasil invois ASAL (CR_SALES jika invois kredit, CASH_SALES jika invois tunai) + DR OUTPUT_TAX (jika cukai > 0) / CR anak akaun debtor pelanggan |
| 11 | **Nota Debit (DN)** | Menambah jumlah yang perlu dibayar pelanggan pada invois tertentu — contohnya pembetulan bil kurang, caj tambahan. | Dokumen pelarasan jenis `type = 'debit_note'`; songsangan tepat bagi catatan CN. | DR anak akaun debtor pelanggan / CR lejar hasil invois ASAL + CR OUTPUT_TAX (jika cukai > 0) |
| 12 | **Nota Bayaran Balik (RN) — berpasangan dengan Nota Kredit** | Wang dibayar balik secara fizikal kepada pelanggan untuk CN yang telah dikeluarkan (dan bukan dibiarkan sebagai kredit tidak digunakan). | Dokumen pelarasan jenis `type = 'refund_note'` dengan `paired_with_id` menunjuk kepada CN tersebut. Tidak menyentuh hasil jualan langsung — hanya mengeluarkan tunai/bank dan menyelesaikan kesan CN pada baki debtor. | DR anak akaun debtor pelanggan / CR akaun TUNAI atau BANK (mengikut `refund_method`/`bank_account`) |
| 13 | **Nota Bayaran Balik (RN) — berdiri sendiri, untuk bayaran lebih** | Pelanggan pernah membayar lebih (senario 8) dan kini mahu lebihan itu dibayar balik secara tunai/bank dan bukan digunakan untuk invois akan datang. | RN berdiri sendiri dengan `linked_payment_id` menunjuk kepada resit/bayaran lebihan asal. `receipt_allocations.refunded_amount` yang berkaitan ditambah supaya baki lebihan mencerminkan bayaran balik itu. | DR CUST_DEP (lebihan) / CR akaun TUNAI atau BANK |
| 14 | **Penukaran jenis bayaran (kredit ↔ tunai)** | Pengguna menukar invois daripada kredit kepada tunai atau sebaliknya selepas ia dicipta (jarang berlaku, tetapi disokong untuk pembetulan). | Jurnal invois dibatalkan dan dibina semula mengikut kontrak `paymenttype` baharu (2 baris untuk kredit, sehingga 4 baris untuk tunai); mana-mana baris auto-collection dicipta/dibuang mengikut keperluan. | Dibina semula mengikut Senario 1 atau 3 di atas, bergantung kepada jenis baharu |
| 15 | **Catatan bank manual yang tidak berkaitan dengan mana-mana invois** | Pergerakan tunai pelbagai melalui bank — contohnya bayaran balik pinjaman pekerja diterima, bayaran balik pembekal diterima, caj bank, faedah — yang tidak berkaitan dengan invois jualan. | Direkod sebagai catatan RV manual/pelbagai dalam `rv_registry` yang dikongsi (kumpulan penomboran yang sama dengan Cash Bank-In berstruktur), atau sebagai catatan jurnal manual berdiri sendiri (`entry_type = 'J'`) jika nombor RV tidak diperlukan. | DR/CR akaun bank terhadap kod akaun sebenar yang terlibat (contohnya DR BANK_PBB / CR akaun pinjaman staf) |

### Notes / Nota

- **CH_REV1** = physical cash collected from CASH bills, held as undeposited cash until banked in. / Tunai fizikal dikutip daripada bil TUNAI, disimpan sebagai tunai belum dibank sehingga di-bank-in.
- **CH_REV2** = physical cash collected as a standalone receipt against a CREDIT invoice, held as undeposited cash until banked in. / Tunai fizikal dikutip sebagai resit berasingan untuk invois KREDIT, disimpan sebagai tunai belum dibank sehingga di-bank-in.
- **CASH_SALES / CR_SALES** = revenue ledgers for cash-bill sales and credit-invoice sales respectively. / Lejar hasil untuk jualan bil tunai dan jualan invois kredit masing-masing.
- **CUST_DEP** = customer deposit account holding unapplied overpayment/excess, usable against future invoices or refundable. / Akaun deposit pelanggan yang menyimpan bayaran lebih/lebihan belum digunakan, boleh digunakan untuk invois akan datang atau dibayar balik.
- Every customer has their own **debtor-child account** (under the `DEBTOR` parent code, code = customer id) instead of one shared control account — this lets the Customer Statement and Trial Balance both work from the same ledger. / Setiap pelanggan mempunyai **akaun anak debtor** sendiri (di bawah kod induk `DEBTOR`, kod = id pelanggan) dan bukan satu akaun kawalan dikongsi — ini membolehkan Penyata Pelanggan dan Imbangan Duga kedua-duanya berfungsi daripada lejar yang sama.
